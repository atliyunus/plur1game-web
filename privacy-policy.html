Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Daily Puzzle mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Our application collects minimal data to improve the user experience. We do not require or story personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, etc.).

  • Device Information: Technical information such as device model and operating system version may be collected to monitor app performance.
  • Usage Data: Your in-app interactions (puzzles solved, game times) may be analyzed anonymously.

2. How We Use Information

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To ensure the App functions correctly.
  • To identify and fix bugs.
  • To enhance the user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for analytics or ad serving. These services are subject to their own privacy policies.

4. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
